How the network actually works

01

You run a node

Install once on any machine. Your node joins the global mesh and advertises its location and capabilities.

02

AI tasks arrive

Compute tasks enter the shared mempool and are dispatched to nearby nodes. Your machine picks them up automatically — no action needed.

03

Your LLM solves it. You earn.

Your local LLM runs inference on the task. First valid result from the fastest node wins the POH reward — low latency and good hardware win more.

What is PoH Miner, really?

AI needs compute. But that compute doesn't have to live in a data center owned by a single company.

PoH Miner is a decentralized AI compute network. You run a node on your own hardware. When an AI task arrives, your local LLM processes it right on your device. You earn POH for every task solved.

No cloud. No middleman. Your machine does real AI work for the network — and the network pays you for it.
