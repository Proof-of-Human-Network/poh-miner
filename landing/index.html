How the network actually works

01

You run a node

Install once on any machine. Your node joins the global mesh and advertises its location and capabilities.

02

Real jobs arrive

When someone requests a digital identity scan on proofofhuman.ge, the job enters the shared mempool with geographic metadata.

03

You win or lose the race

Low-latency nodes near the requester have a natural advantage. First valid result using the real POH brain wins the reward in POH.

What is PoH Miner, really?

The internet is being flooded with AI. Proving that a real human is behind an action is becoming one of the most important problems of our time.

PoH Miner is the decentralized compute network that powers Proof of Human — the most advanced open system for verifying humans vs machines at scale.

Instead of wasting electricity on meaningless math, miners run real AI models and sophisticated signal checks on actual user requests. The better and faster they do the real work, the more they earn.
